Enabling a sustainable world

Sustainability is at the heart of our research – we’ve invested more than $30 million in sustainability-related research in 2023 alone. Our researchers are focused on innovative local and global sustainability solutions.

Facilities

  1. AquaFI Lab – sustainable fish production and novel feed and aquaculture technology development.
  2. Centre for Regional and Rural Futures – water management, sensing technologies and environmental engineering facilities.
  3. HYCEL – new hydrogen technologies for clean energy and transport innovation
  4. Integrated Water Management – network of ponds, constructed wetland and irrigation systems.

Designing smarter technologies

We collaborate with partners and HDR researchers to develop human-centered technologies that address real-world challenges, while prioritising the wellbeing of our planet and society.

Facilities

  1. Battery Research and Innovation Hub – advanced battery prototyping and commercialisation.
  2. Carbon Nexus – prototyping facility that supports design, fabrication and testing for next-generation batteries.
  3. Genesis Simulator – immersive simulation experience via a 360-degree, 5-metre-high screen and variable virtual worlds.
  4. Institute for Frontier Materials – carbon fibre and composite research facility.
